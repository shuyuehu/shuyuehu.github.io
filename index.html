Hello, I'm Shuyue.

I’m a researcher at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, Shanghai, China. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. at the Chinese University of Hong Kong and was a postdoctoral research fellow at Singapore University of Technology and Design and National University of Singapore.

My research interests are in multi-agent systems, game theory, and large language models.
