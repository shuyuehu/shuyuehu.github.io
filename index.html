Hello, I'm Shuyue.

I'm a researcher at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory in Shanghai, China. My research interests include, but are not limited to, multi-agent systems, large language models, and game theory.

Currently, my focus is on the intersection of large language models and multi-agent systems. Specifically, I am interested in: (i) improving the performance of LLMs by applying theories, principles, and methodologies from multi-agent systems, and (ii) using LLMs to address the open challenges within multi-agent systems.
